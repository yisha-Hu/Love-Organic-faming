why should you support organic farming?

Health Benefits

Organic farming benefits our health in many ways, one of the most significant benefits is reducing exposure to harmful chemicals, it also reducing synthetic fertilizers and pesticides link to cancer, congenital diabilities and other health problems. This benefits everyone including children who are still developing their immune systems and maybe more susceptible to the effects of pesticides than adults

Environmental Benefits

Organic farming benefits our enviroment as it is much more sustainable than traditional farming, the practices of organic farming rely on natural inputs like compost and manure instead of synthetic fertilizers and pesticides, this means that organic farming reducces fossil fuel consumpiton thus reducing pollution in our air and water which linked to climate change.

Community Benifits

Organic Farming is important part of local community, it contributes to local food supply and provides local economic impact. Also volunteering has the impact on the community, unpaid Volunteers are often the glue that holds a community together, in return Volunteering help you to make new friends and contacts, increasing social and relationship skills, self-confidence and conbating depression, helps you stay healthy.
